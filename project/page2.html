Walking your dog is not just a routine task—it’s a crucial part of their well-being. Here are some key benefits:

  • Bonding Time: Walking together strengthens the bond between you and your pet (ASPCA, 2023).
  • Mental Stimulation: Exploring new sights and smells keeps your dog engaged and prevents boredom (PetMD, 2023).
  • Behavioral Improvement: Dogs that get enough exercise are less likely to develop destructive behaviors such as excessive barking or chewing (AKC, 2023).
  • Physical Health: Regular walks help maintain a healthy weight, strengthen muscles, and improve cardiovascular health (CDC, 2023).
